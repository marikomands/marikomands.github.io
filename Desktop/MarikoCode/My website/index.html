Gluten Free Bread

Gluten Free bread could be sometimes very hard and not fluffy like regular bread. After experiencing many gluten free bread making, I have came up with the recipies of the best gluten free bread. Also, I have noticed many differencebetween Japanese and Western gluten free bread recipies. In japan, we mainly use rice flour for gluten free bread (yes, Japanese people love rice very much!) Unlike western recipies, the recipies don't inclde any eggs. white eggs are full of protein and tend to make a bread hard, so it might be because of that.
